Creamy Pasta Alfredo
Ingredients:
  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
Spicy Chicken Tikka
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, tikka masala, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and salt for 2 hours.
  2. Grill the chicken until fully cooked and charred.
  3. Serve with naan or rice.
Vegetable Stir Fry
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
Instructions:
  1. Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a large pan or wok.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, and cornstarch. Pour over vegetables.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens. Serve hot.
Juicy Beef Burgers
Ingredients:
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments for serving
Instructions:
  1. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill or pan-fry patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. Toast burger buns and assemble with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments.
  5. Serve immediately.
Decadent Chocolate Cake
Ingredients:
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour into pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Cool completely before frosting.
Spicy Shrimp Tacos
Ingredients:
  • 500g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, paprika, cayenne, and garlic powder.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until pink.
  3. Warm tortillas and fill with shrimp, cabbage, tomatoes, and cilantro.
  4. Serve with lime wedges.
Vegetable Lasagna
Ingredients:
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta, spinach, mushrooms, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Layer marinara sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
Fresh Fruit Salad
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1 kiwi, peeled and sliced
  • 1 orange, peeled and segmented
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
Instructions:
  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey and lime juice.
  3. Toss gently to combine. Serve chilled.
Margherita Pizza
Ingredients:
  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough. Top with mozzarella slices.
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
  4. Top with fresh basil, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
